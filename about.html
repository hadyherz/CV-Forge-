Our Story

Built in Egypt, For the World

CV Forge was born from a simple frustration — talented people getting filtered out by software before any human ever reads their work.

The Problem We Saw

Every week, thousands of qualified job seekers in Egypt and the Middle East apply for positions they're more than capable of filling — and hear nothing back. Not because they're unqualified. Because their resume was rejected by a machine before anyone read it.

Most resume builders available online are either expensive subscriptions priced for US markets, produce image-based "pretty" resumes that ATS systems can't parse, or provide no guidance on what ATS systems actually need to see.

CV Forge exists to fix all three of those problems — for a fraction of the cost.

"We wanted to make professional CV tools accessible to anyone in Egypt and the MENA region — not just people who can afford expensive subscriptions, and not just people who are already professional copywriters."

— The CV Forge Team, Egypt

What We Built

CV Forge is a web application that helps anyone build an ATS-optimized resume in minutes. No design skills. No copywriting background. No expensive subscription.

We built 5 professional templates specifically engineered to pass ATS filters. We added AI writing prompts that guide you through every section — so even if English isn't your first language, you can write like a professional. We made it work perfectly on phones, because most of our users are on mobile.

And we priced it honestly: $1.99 one-time, lifetime access. No recurring charges, no subscriptions.
